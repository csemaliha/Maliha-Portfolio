Focus Areas

Research Interests

My research centers on Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) — developing techniques that make machine learning models more transparent and interpretable to the people who rely on them. Alongside this, I work on IoT security, data processing pipelines, and end-to-end machine learning workflows, with an emphasis on translating raw, messy data into reliable, well-understood systems.
